Black Lotus Italia

Black Lotus Yoga arose unexpectedly through Ty Landrum’s deep devotion to Ashtanga, evolving during the quiet of the global lockdown. What began as a spontaneous shift in his personal practice soon revealed itself as a new form of vinyasa, merging breath, movement, and creative energy in an entirely fresh way. This practice, rooted in tradition yet pushing beyond its boundaries, now stands as an offering to others—an exploration of the body’s natural intelligence and the sacred connection between movement and breath.
